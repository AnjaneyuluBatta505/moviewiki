Prabhas Biography

Prabhas was born to Suryanarayana Raju and Siva Kumari in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. His family is from Mogalturu village in West Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh. He is the youngest of three children. He has an elder brother Pramod, who is a tollywood producer and a sister Pragathee. He is the nephew of famous tollywood actor and politician Krishnam Raju. He has acquired the title "Young Rebel Star" from the prominent actor Krishnam Raju's title "Rebel Star". Career Prabhas has started his acting career with the movie 'Eeshwar' but gained recognition with the blockbuster hit film 'Varsham'. He was also seen in the family entertrainer 'Mirchi', opposite stunning actress Anushka Shetty. The film became super hit and has got great applauses from the audience. He has also rendered voice for a short cameo for the film Denikaina Ready. He came up with a big budget film "Baahubali" and ready for his upcoming "Baahubali-part 2". Prabhas is the brand ambassador for Mahindra TUV 300 Car. Prabhas has became a huge person in the ad world. Girlfriend/Marriage Prabhas is one of the most eligible bachelors of Telugu filmdom. There are several talks about the actors marriage. Now his uncle, noted actor Krishnam Raju revealed that, he has met Prabhas and took a promise from him that he will get married in 2016. Prabhas seems to have readily agreed as well. They will now start looking for a suitable bride for Prabhas. There is also speculation that he will get married once he completes Baahubali part 2 for which they are shooting this year.
